Fun Facts

As someone who values living life to the fullest, I make sure to make the most out of my free time by doing what I love the most. I have a deep appreciation for sports, especially snowboarding and hiking, which give me the chance to enjoy the beautiful outdoors and challenge myself physically.

Cooking is another passion of mine, particularly preparing Szechuan-style Chinese food. I love experimenting with different ingredients and recipes, and the satisfaction I get from creating a delicious meal is incomparable.

I enjoy playing League of Legends in my spare time as well. It's a great way to unwind and challenge myself mentally. The competitive aspect of the game keeps me on my toes, and I love the sense of community it brings.
